Full Western is a photography book that examines the intricacies of tennis between the points. The project is a collection of photographs exploring the overlooked elements of the game: the stringer, the court signage, the communities of players challenging tennis’s long history of wealth and exclusivity, and the quiet moments that often go unnoticed. Together, these images offer a broader portrait of the sport.
74 pages. 6 in x 9 in. Perfect bound. Hand-stamped front cover. Vellum dust jacket.
Photography: Jacob Arvidson
Art Direction & Layout Design: Leigh Annand, Jacob Arvidson
Editor: Jay Boss Rubin, Scott Korb
Foreword: Tyler Pell
Shot on Pentax Honeywell Spotmatic and Konica Big Mini-201/ Porta 400, Kodak Gold 200 and HP5 35mm film.
